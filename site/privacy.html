PRIVACY

Your numbers should not need a passport.

The Quick Stress Test performs calculations locally in your browser. It does not require a backend, cookies, analytics, trackers, hidden lead capture, or automatic transmission of entered financial data.

Data you should not enter

Do not enter Social Security numbers, passwords, full bank account numbers, card data, API keys, authentication codes, or private credentials.

Local drafts

No draft is saved automatically. When you explicitly select “Save this draft on this device,” the tool uses your browser’s localStorage. You can delete that draft with the visible “Delete Local Draft” control or clear browser site data.

Exports

Markdown, CSV, JSON, copy, and print exports are generated in your browser. Review and redact exports before sharing them.

External links

Opening the lead magnet, Moonshine Capital, a partner pathway, or the full Custom GPT takes you to a separate service with its own privacy practices.
